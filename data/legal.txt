LEGAL NOTICE

This agreement is made and executed on this day between the parties herein.
Whereas the Donor is the absolute owner of the property and desires to transfer
the said property to the Donee out of natural love and affection.

The Donor hereby transfers, conveys, assigns and assures the said property,
together with all rights, title, interest, easements and appurtenances.

This deed shall be binding on the parties, their heirs, executors, administrators
and legal representatives.

LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT

I, being of sound mind and good health, declare this to be my last will.
I hereby revoke all previous wills and testaments made by me earlier.

I appoint an executor to carry out the instructions mentioned herein.
In case of dispute, the jurisdiction shall be governed by the laws of India.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have signed this document in the presence of witnesses.
